HotelAdvisor is an application made for gathering more information about hotels in the city of Dubrovnik. It is very easy to use, with simple but effective design. Because of its simplicity, people from all ages can use it. It is made mostly for young people (age range from 25-40) and for older ones (age range: 40 and more) because these generations are already working and have stable income. HotelAdvisor is different from other similar applications because it is more user experience and it is easy to use.
