HotelAdvisor application is easy to use! Side menu is consisting of home page with details about hotel, picture of hotel, more info and location. In home section you can find a list of hotels in Dubrovnik and when you click on them, it leads you to a page where is description of your chosen hotel, together with other information. In about section you can find some introduction to application and finally in How to use part, here is short guide for using this application. Everytime user clicks to see more about hotel, he is able to view his current location and route to specific hotel. Also, if the user is intersted in more information, ge can click on the button 'More info' and he will be redirected on official site of Hotel.
